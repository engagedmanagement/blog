A New Output Format

html_pretty in the prettydoc package is a new output format for creating HTML document from R Markdown files. html_pretty is more lightweight compared to html_document, and is more stylish than html_vignette when creating package vignettes.

Preview your post

Use the Knit button of RStudio to preview your post in HTML format. You should have a very similar rendering for the main content of your post when it will be on your blog.

Convert your post

Use prettyjekyll::FormatPost to convert your post.
